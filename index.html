Travel responsibly in Sri Lanka

Respect Nature

  • Do not litter
  • Avoid damaging ecosystems
  • Follow local guidelines

Support Local Communities

  • Hire local guides
  • Buy from small businesses
  • Respect local culture

Protect Wildlife

  • Avoid unethical animal tourism
  • Keep safe distance

Reduce Carbon Impact

  • Use public transport
  • Travel in groups
  • Avoid plastic
